About Us

Triple S Essentials is Ikorodu's trusted name in fashion and creative design. Located in the heart of Agric, Owutu, we specialize in transforming fabric into art. Our team of skilled designers and tailors combine traditional craftsmanship with contemporary trends to deliver fashion that speaks to your personality.

Whether you need custom tailoring, ready-to-wear pieces, or creative design consultation, we are committed to exceeding your expectations with every stitch. We pride ourselves on attention to detail, timely delivery, and creating lasting relationships with our clients across Ikorodu and beyond.
